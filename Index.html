About the Vikings

The Minnesota Vikings, established in 1961, are one of the most storied franchises in the NFL. Known for their fierce defense, passionate fans, and the iconic horn sound that echoes through U.S. Bank Stadium, the Vikings have built a rich legacy over the decades.

The team’s signature purple and gold colors, along with their Norse-inspired logo, reflect the strength and heritage of the region. Despite heartbreaks in several Super Bowls and NFC Championship games, the Vikings remain one of the league’s most beloved teams.
